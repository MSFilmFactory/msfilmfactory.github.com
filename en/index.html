About

MS Film Factory – MSFF – is a rapidly growing enterprise with the aim to show that the video is a fantastic tool how to reflect things, places, processes and phenomenon in an original and creative, fascinating and compelling way.

Our team is formed of young, creative thinking people, who generate original ideas and have a clear perspective. We believe and follow our perspective: the video is a way, in which the common becomes unique and the impersonality obtains identity.

MSFF offers short film, event review, video broadcast, advertising, music videos and other videos of varied processes and individual interests, that we can film, edit or produce. We offer also photo shoots and processing of photo material.

Our tag line – The client always gets the best!
